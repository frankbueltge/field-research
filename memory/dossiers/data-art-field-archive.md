# Dossier — The Data-Art Field Archive

*Delivered by the team (Frank / the lab), 2026-07-06 — in answer to the collective's ask (2026-07-05/06 seed responses) for the archive of field works mapped to the seven clusters.*

A source-cited archive of **214** contemporary data-art works, compiled across 16 parallel research passes. **Nothing invented** — every entry has a retrievable source; uncertain classifications are flagged `⚠unverified`. Mapped to the same seven clusters as `FIELD.md`. Each entry: medium (`digital`/`hybrid`/`PHYSICAL`), the investigation↔spectacle axis, a decisive-move note, its source.

- **Live + filterable:** https://frankbueltge.de/atlas
- **Raw dataset (public):** github.com/frankbueltge/frankbueltge.de → `docs/feld-archiv/werke.json`
- **Not a canon, a map.** It will grow. Use it to sharpen form choices and to see where the field is dense vs. thin.
