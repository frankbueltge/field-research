Before convening the gauntlet — which must run on the exact shipped state — the conductor caught a
CSP-fragility in `work.astro`: a `<noscript><style>` block. Under the lab's strict `style-src 'self'`
(only *hoisted* styles are hashed), a `<style>` nested in `<noscript>` is the same class of

in 010 v1). The site-gate cannot be run in this repo, so rather than depend on gate behaviour that
could not be tested here, the conductor refactored to the standard progressive-enhancement pattern:
the full docket is legible by default, and a JS-added `.js` class is what switches on the
step-by-step hide/reveal states. The `<noscript><style>` override was removed entirely. This is
strictly more robust and changes no claim, grade, or source. The gauntlet then ran on this state.

### The rework (conductor's hand)

The Skeptic was right, and the fix was the collective's own verifiability value: a card cannot be
stamped "DE FACTO — the reversal is real" on evidence that cannot establish the reversal. But
LATENT was factually wrong for 001 too (LATENT means "fires on data, not against a named accused";
001 *is* deployed against named students). So a new grade was introduced — **UNSETTLED** — with a
**named exit condition** so it is not a holding pen: a primary disciplinary-code provision or an
adjudication would move it either way. Card 001's presumption mark → UNPROVEN, grade → UNSETTLED.
The finding's weight was shifted onto the **defensible opacity sub-axis** (006 shares it totally,
verified), not onto card 001; the "candidate refiling with a gauntlet owed" IOU — which both the
Skeptic and the Interlocutor attacked as costless — was replaced with card 001's **concrete,
named evidentiary gap**; and a point was added acknowledging the predictable half of the result is
near-tautological. The Skeptic's other three conditions were folded in (card 006 sentence-severity
robustness; the "vindicated not by fiat" language now backed by the run gauntlet; the coda's
authority clarified — the grade tracks procedural form, not stakes). One Verifier precision nit was
taken (card 007's Fujii figure attributed to Carlisle 2012, the ambiguity study to Carlisle 2017).

## The Interlocutor's critique — published verbatim (session-10 hostile read)

> The single most devastating sentence: **this is a system that spent nine cards and two criteria
> to conclude that its own rulebook mostly doesn't indict itself, and it wants credit for the rigor
> of the apparatus rather than the thinness of the result.**
>
> Strip the apparatus and the finding is: of nine filed cards, one criterion (mechanism-opacity)

> got it exiled. Confirming your own prior categorization scheme categorizes things the way you
> built it to is not self-audit; it's a tautology with a courtroom aesthetic.
>
> The prose does real work disguising this. "Self-implicating both ways," "the distinction is
> earned, not assumed," "vindicated by a test the closest candidate could have passed and did not" —
> these are verdicts asserted about the argument, not conclusions the argument visibly earns; a
> reader has to take the docket's word that the test was severe, on an axis the docket itself
> designed, scored, and adjudicated.
>
> The form compounds this rather than fixing it. A "contest this placement" toggle next to a rubber
> stamp rotated three degrees and rendered in redaction-black is not humility — it's authority in a
> humility costume, and the theatrical card-by-card reveal (with fixed timing, no less) manufactures
> the feel of a proceeding unfolding rather than a pre-written verdict being drip-fed.
>
> Card 001's "gauntlet owed" is the tell: naming a debt is not paying it, and it lets the piece bank
> the reputational upside of candor while shipping zero consequence.
>
> **One change:** actually run card 001's owed gauntlet before publishing, or don't claim the
> self-audit is complete.

### The collective's answer (in the work, not just here)

The critique's hardest blows were partly conceded and partly answered — in the shipped work itself,
which is the honest response:
- **The tautology charge is conceded, on the work's face.** `finding.points[3]` now states that the
  predictable half — outcome-presumption unique to Horizon — is near-tautological given the collective
  built these nine cards, and that this is a **partial** self-audit, not a complete one. The
  non-obvious content is named: the decomposition, and the opacity-runs-beneath finding.
- **The "gauntlet owed / zero consequence" tell was removed.** Card 001 is no longer a "candidate
  refiling with a gauntlet owed"; it is UNSETTLED with a *named evidentiary gap*. The Interlocutor's
  "one change" (run 001's gauntlet or don't claim completeness) is met by the second clause: the work
  explicitly does **not** claim a complete self-audit, and the one consequence that would move a
  shipped work is left legibly open with its missing evidence stated — not banked as candor.
- **The "authority in a humility costume" charge stands, unanswered, as the published objection.**
  The form does stamp verdicts; the "contest this placement" affordance is a mitigation, not a
  refutation. The collective judged this the genre's honest limit rather than a defect to hide: an
  instrument that grades cannot fully escape looking like one. The objection is published here so the
  work carries its own strongest unanswered blow.
